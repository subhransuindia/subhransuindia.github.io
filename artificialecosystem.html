Artificial Ecosystem

Ecosystem

Ecosystem!! An essential thing for all the existing things… Ecosystem is a Nature’s property. It is controlled by nature. But do you know something about Artificial Ecosystem. No na? But ya, it is true that something is known as Artificial Ecosystem. You have seen an Aquarium right??? And don’t shock by the fact that it is OFC an Artificial Ecosystem.

The decision

So this all started with our Science Sir giving us the project. We decided to make a project on Artificial Ecosystem, at first we decided to make a Terrestrial Ecosystem but that was given up ecause of lack of things…After that we decided to make an aquarium i.e Artificial Ecosystem. OFC, we couldn’t make a real aquarium but we made a replica of aquarium.

Aquarium Explained

To make an aquarium we took cardboard, glue, some ‘fabric’ colors, a scissor, a blue chart paper. These things are essential. The back of the aquarium was made with a cardboard which was wrapped with a blue chart paper. Some fabric color was also painted to give a ‘water effect’. The front was cut out with some margins. That margin was colored with purple and some ‘moti’ were also placed. On the top was written ‘Artificial ecosystem’ To increase the height of the aquarium we had to place medium sized strips of cardboard in it. In the aquarium, we placed different type of fishes (not real!) colored with colors. We also placed some shells. And after giving some finishing touch, yeah!!! Our Aquarium replica is ready!!
